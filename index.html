Welcome to Central Training Institute

"Our focus is not just creating drivers or mechanics, but constructing a force of highly skilled, responsible professionals who ensure safety, efficiency, and exceptional service on public roads."

For over four decades, KSRTC Central Training Institute has acted as a lighthouse of professional human resource development for the transport sector. By integrating virtual simulator driving, modern mechanic lab practices, and EV diagnostics, we prepare our personnel to meet the standards of tomorrow's green logistics.
