About Our Institute

Four decades of training excellence, preparing skilled professionals for safe public transit.

Serving Karnataka with Professional Skills Since 1980

KSRTC Central Training Institute (CTI) is the premium training entity owned and operated by the Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation.

Established to elevate safety standards, technical know-how, and public service values, CTI has trained over 50,000 transport personnel. The institute incorporates virtual interactive simulators, high-precision driving tracks, and dedicated electrical/diesel engine labs to train drivers, conductors, mechanics, and managers in key transport systems.

ISO 9001:2015 Academy
National Safety Award

Our Vision

To become a world-class training center of excellence that establishes the highest standards of safety, passenger convenience, environmental responsibility, and technical capability in the Indian public transport sector.

Our Mission

  • Implement modern training methods, including virtual simulators and computerized performance diagnostics.
  • Inculcate positive values of commuter relations, soft communication, and empathy among transport workers.
  • Advance skills in electric mobility, alternative fuels, and modern automated logistics.

Our Milestone Journey

A timeline of our progressive expansion and service milestones over the decades.

1980
CTI Foundation

Inaugurated as the central human resource development center at Shantinagar, Bengaluru, under the Department of Transport.

1996
Automated Skill Track

CTI Bangalore launched the state's first dedicated automated heavy motor vehicle testing track with height sensors and hill-climbing modules.

2010
Regional Decentralization

Established Driver and Technical Training centers in Malavalli (Mandya) and Hassan, expanding standard training modules across Karnataka.

2018
Simulator Diagnostics

Integrated computerized virtual-reality driving simulators to train drivers in high-risk road environments without diesel consumption.

2026
Green Mobility Transition

Launched dedicated diagnostics and repair labs for Electric Vehicles (EVs) and smart batteries at Hubli and Bangalore institutes.

CTI Training Infrastructure

Explore the physical assets and teaching environments that empower our trainees.

Virtual Driving Lab

Equipped with standard-sized bus dashboards, realistic gear levers, air-brakes, and surround display screens to analyze response time.

Automobile Workstations

Clean workshop bays where ITI trainees dismantle and re-assemble actual bus chassis, hydraulic steering, and multi-speed gearboxes.

Interactive Smart Rooms

Equipped with digital presentation boards, road sign mockups, and computerized ticketing machines for interactive conductor training.
